General Questions

How do I book a flight?

You can book a flight by navigating to login/signup and entering your travel details.

Can I cancel or change my flight?

Yes, you can cancel or change your flight through the My Profile page or by contacting customer support.

What is your baggage policy?

We allow one carry-on and one checked bag per passenger. Additional baggage fees may apply. Please review the Baggage Policy for more information.
